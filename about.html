Our Story

Established in 2001 by the Bhatta brothers, Mahakali Bijuli Bhandar (MBB) has grown from a small electrical shop in Dhangadhi Chauraha to one of the most trusted electrical suppliers in the Sudurpaschim region.

We specialize in sourcing directly from reputable manufacturers to bring our customers the best products at competitive prices. Our extensive inventory includes everything from basic electrical components to advanced industrial solutions.
